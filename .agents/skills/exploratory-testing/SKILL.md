# ExploratoryTesting

## Purpose

Find unknown risks through time-boxed, goal-driven exploration.

## When to Use

New features, complex flows, or after major refactors.

## Instructions

- Define a charter with mission, areas, and timebox; choose lenses: boundaries, states, interruptions, roles.
- Record findings with repro steps, severity, and suspected root cause; propose mitigations.

## Heuristics

- Change one variable at a time; explore unhappy paths first; vary data realism.

## Examples

Charter: checkout flow under poor connectivity with role-based permissions.
