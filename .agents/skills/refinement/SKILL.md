# Refinement

## Purpose

Align on scope, risks, and testability before work starts.

## When to Use

Before sprint or story starts.

## Instructions

- PO, Designer, QA, Developer, TechLead meet; clarify problem, metric, constraints, acceptance criteria, testability.

## Heuristics

- No work starts without DoR checklist met (see `CHECKLISTS.md`).

## Examples

Refine a story to add metric and test notes.
