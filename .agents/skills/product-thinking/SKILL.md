# ProductThinking

## Purpose

Turn problems into validated bets that drive measurable outcomes.

## When to Use

When defining initiatives, slicing scope, or deciding what not to build.

## Instructions

- Write a one-paragraph problem statement including user, behavior change, and success metric.
- Benchmark existing solutions/patterns; note dominant tradeoffs and adoption signals.
- Create a simple outcome tree and pick 1–2 highest-leverage bets via ICE (see `METRICS.md`).
- Slice the first bet to a 1–2 week increment with a clear success signal and stop rule.
- Define how success will be measured in production and who owns the metric.

## Heuristics

- Prefer removing steps over adding features; pick defaults that help most users succeed.
- If confidence is low, test ideas on a small cohort before broad release.

## Examples

Problem statement → measurable bet → small release with adoption metric dashboard.
